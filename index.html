About Fruit Juice

Fruit juice is the liquid from fruit, packed with vitamins, antioxidants, and natural sugars. It offers benefits such as hydration and skin health. However, moderation is important due to concentrated sugars and calories. 100% fruit juice is healthier than juice drinks with added sugars. While juice is a convenient way to get nutrients, whole fruits remain superior because of their fiber content. Consuming fruit juice along with whole fruits and vegetables is recommended for overall health.
